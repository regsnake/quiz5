WHAT DRIVES GLOBAL ENTREPRENEURS?

Entrepreneurs strive to make difference in our world and to contribute to its betterment.
They identify opportunies ,mobilize resources ,and relentlessly execute on their visions.
Entrepreneurs act to create new enterprise.We identify firms as key structures in the economy and the role of entrepreneurship as the engine of economic growth .
New technologies form the basis of many important ventures where scientists and engineers combine their technical knowlegde with sound businesses practices to foster innovation.

ENTREPRENEURSHIP IN CONTEXT.

Entrepreneurship is more than the creation of a business and the wealth associated with it.
It is focused on the creation of a new enterprice that serves society and makes a positive change.
Entrepreneurs can create great and reputable firms that exhibit performance , leadership and longetivity.
Examples of successive entrepreneurs ,Brin Sergey-Google,Dorsey-Twitter ,Bezos-Amazon.com e.t.c.
